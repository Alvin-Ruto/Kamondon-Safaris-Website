Destination guide

Maasai Mara National Reserve

Kenya's iconic safari landscape for Big Five wildlife, sweeping plains, and the Great Migration.

Why visit

Wildlife density, open scenery, and unforgettable game drives.

The Maasai Mara is ideal for first-time safari travelers, photographers, families, couples, and groups looking for reliable wildlife viewing and classic Kenya safari storytelling.

  • Big cats, elephants, buffalo, giraffe, zebra, and antelope.
  • Great Migration river crossings in peak season.
  • Wide accommodation range from budget camps to luxury lodges.
  • Easy to combine with Lake Nakuru, Amboseli, Diani, or Nairobi stays.

Plan the right season

Best Time To Visit

The Mara is excellent year-round. July to October is popular for the migration, while quieter months can offer strong value and fewer crowds.
