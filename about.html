About Kamondon Safaris

A Kenya-based tours and travel company built around one promise: travel made easy and effortless.

Our story

Warm, practical, and deeply local travel support.

Kamondon Safaris helps local and international customers plan safaris, hotel bookings, airport transfers, ticketing, excursions, event planning, budget safaris, luxury safaris, corporate trips, and team-building experiences.

  • Mission: make Kenya travel clear, exciting, and well organized.
  • Vision: become a trusted travel partner for safari, coast, corporate, and custom Kenya experiences.
  • Values: clarity, care, flexibility, local expertise, and reliable communication.
